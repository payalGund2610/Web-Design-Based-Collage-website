About Us

The college was founded by Samarth Rural Educational Institute, Rajuri, in 2014. The college is affiliated to the Savitribai Phule Pune University, Ganeshkhind, Pune 411007. The college provides various academic amenities so as to attain Bachelors degrees in the fields of Computer Science. The college is a reputed educational institution, which has been known for producing outstanding students who take on different careers, as per their academic merit, successfully in the society. Various educational authorities have noted the achievements and reputation of the college. The Govt. of Maharashtra and Savitribai Phule Pune University have recently accorded the sanction to increase the intake capacity of the B. Sc. Course. The young and highly qualified team of faculty members spare no efforts to keep the students well informed and in updating their knowledge.
